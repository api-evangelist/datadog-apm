This post explains how to migrate to Azure Managed Redis using Datadog and the Eden tool to establish performance baselines, execute safe cutovers, and continuously monitor cache health throughout the migration. The combination of observability and automation reduces downtime risk when transitioning high-traffic Redis workloads.
